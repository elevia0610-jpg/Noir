artifex

Typeface design
with AI Support

Artifex is a web-based AI tool that simplifies font design by automatically generating characters, ensuring consistency while enabling creative variation.

By automating repetitive tasks, Artifex lets designers focus on creativity and experiment with new styles.

Logo

The prototype introduces Artifex on a landing page, showcasing its key features. Full interaction with the AI, including testing its character handling and generating fonts, is available after registration through a simple dashboard where users can select their desired actions.

Users can upload either system-recommended or custom-selected characters, review and approve generated glyphs, make edits, regenerate if needed, and download the final designs as font files or SVGs, supporting an iterative and flexible workflow.

Artifex is a conceptual project with a proof of concept demonstrating potential AI-assisted font design workflows. The research and design log documents hypotheses, testing, and insights into the model's behavior and user interactions.
